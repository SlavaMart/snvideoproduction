Our services

What We do?

Our team of professional videographers creates great videos for business in social media, taking into account the individual characteristics and needs of each client.

View all services

Pre-production

Involves developing a shooting idea, writing a script, inspecting the location, selecting actors, calculating estimates and agreeing on all the details of the shooting.

Post production

Creating a media product. At this stage, editing, color correction, adding effects and much more necessary to create the final video are carried out.

Production

Depending on the complexity of the project, we invite the required number of film crew members to implement the project according to a pre-agreed scenario.

Completion of the project

We hand over the finished video material. We provide recommendations for posting videos on platforms chosen by the client and help resolve all related issues.
