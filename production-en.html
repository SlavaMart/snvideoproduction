Home Services

Advertising video

Short videos that show your product to the client. Aimed at selling goods and services, as well as promoting the company in the market.

Reporting video events

Reporting videos about an event that took place, in order to show what the event was like. Used for conferences, seminars, corporate events, advertising campaigns, competitions, exhibitions.

Documentary video reports

This is a combination of reportage and staged video shooting, as well as the use of archival materials to tell the true story that happened.

Music video

Created to convey the atmosphere and visual embodiment of the song. Important elements include the script, choreography, set design, costumes and special effects. They are a great way to promote an artist and song, as well as a source of inspiration.

Interview

A popular format for communication between several people in the frame to tell the story of the hero of the shooting.

Aerial Videos

A popular service both for personal purposes and for public events. They are used to show the real size and location of objects, the mass nature of events, or non-standard shots for various events, for example, weddings.
